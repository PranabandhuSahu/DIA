Digital Inclusivity Auditor

A Digital Inclusivity Auditor is a professional who assesses and ensures that digital products, services, and environments are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. Their role is crucial in promoting digital inclusivity, which aims to eliminate barriers that prevent people from accessing and using digital technologies effectively.

Overall, Digital Inclusivity Auditors play a critical role in fostering a more inclusive digital environment where everyone, regardless of ability, can participate fully and equally in the digital world. Their work contributes to both ethical considerations and legal requirements surrounding accessibility in digital spaces.
